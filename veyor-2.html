HOW BUYING WORKS

From spec to shipment, in four steps

STEP 01

Post your RFQ or search listings

Describe what you need — or let the AI Copilot draft the technical spec for you in seconds.

STEP 02

Get matched & compare quotes

The AI scores nearby suppliers first on trust, price, logistics and quality, then expands regionally, nationally or globally only if local supply falls short.

STEP 03

Negotiate & confirm the order

Message suppliers directly, counter-offer, and lock in terms with Veyor Trade Assurance.

STEP 04

Ship, track & pay

Freight partners handle logistics; payment releases on delivery confirmation.

AI PROCUREMENT COPILOT

Your sourcing team's smartest analyst

The Copilot drafts technical specs, flags risky payment terms, compares supplier quotes line-by-line, and suggests negotiation language — built into every step of the buying flow.

  • ✓ Draft an RFQ from a plain-language description
  • ✓ Score suppliers by proximity, trust, price, logistics & quality
  • ✓ Expand from local → regional → national → global automatically when needed
  • ✓ Compare quotes across price, lead time & Incoterms
  • ✓ Suggest counter-offers and negotiation phrasing
  • ✓ Explain trade terms, tariffs and documentation
